The IB Academy

The IB program's defining mission is to mold young individuals into inquiring, knowledgeable individuals who cultural understanding are able to build a better world

The IB program is an academic program meant for aspiring students from 16-19 years of age who wish to get their IB diploma and become IB scholars. To obtain this diploma, which allows an individual to study abroad and receive significant scholarships. Not only is one able to receive scholarships and study abroad opportunities, recieving an IB diploma shows one's willingness to distinguish themselves from others and accept academic rigor. Alongside AP classes, the IB Diploma along with numerous AP classes is one of the best academic situations.

In order to reap most of the benefits from the IB program, one must get an IB diploma, to get the coveted diploma one must fulfil a variety of tasks and examinations to show your ability to think critically and retain knowledge.The core of the "grade" is based off of the class named Theory of Knowledge (TOK) which test's students ability to think and parse information for what it is and the Extended Essa (EE) a research project about a topic that interests you in apporximetly 4,000 words backed by recognized academic sources from academic journals and reputable books. The bulk of the "grade" is determined by the IB level classes, which divide themselves into SL(Standard Level) and HL (Higher Level), 3 of the HL courses are needed to be taken to get the diploma. The tests have an examination that has 6 diffrents levels, similiar to AP's 5. When one's scores in TOK, the EE and the SL and HL classes are added up, they must equal to about 36.

In the state of Florida most colleges accept IB level courses as credit as long as one passes them, and the majority are wiling to give you 30 credit hours for a 5 in an IB standard level course and a 4 in a higher level IB course. A semester credit hour (SCH) is the amount of credit a student earns for successful completion of one contact hour and two preparation hours per week for a semester. Usually college courses give 15-17 SCHs, making IB classes a worwhile investmen
