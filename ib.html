The IB Academy

The IB program's defining mission is to mold young individuals into inquiring, knowledgeable individuals who cultural understanding are able to build a better world

The IB program is an academic program meant for aspiring students from 16-19 years of age who wish to get their IB diploma and become IB scholars. To obtain this diploma, which allows an individual to study abroad and receive significant scholarships. Not only is one able to receive scholarships and study abroad opportunities, recieving an IB diploma shows one's willingness to distinguish themselves from others and accept academic rigor. Alongside AP classes, the IB Diploma along with numerous AP classes is one of the best academic situations.

In the state of Florida most colleges accept IB level coarses as credit as long as one passes them, and the majority are wiling to give you 30 credit hours for a 5 in an IB standard level coarse and a 4 in a higher level IB coarse. A semester credit hour (SCH) is the amount of credit a student earns for successful completion of one contact hour and two preparation hours per week for a semester.
